Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and our guarantee.

Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

We offer complimentary standard shipping on all domestic orders over $500. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days upon dispatch.

International Shipping

We ship globally to select destinations. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Please allow 7-14 business days for international delivery, depending on customs clearance.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number to monitor your delivery status in real-time.

Returns & Exchanges

Our Guarantee

We want you to love your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, we accept returns on eligible items within 30 days of delivery.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Custom-made or personalized items are final sale and cannot be returned.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns and exchanges.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please visit our Returns Portal or contact our support team. A return shipping label will be provided for domestic returns.
